Can I publish IATI data on behalf of one of my partners?
********************************************************

Generally, we recommend that each organisation is responsible for publishing and maintaining its own IATI data where possible.

If you need to publish on behalf of another organisation, please contact your funder for advice. `The IATI Secretariat <https://iatistandard.org/en/guidance/get-support/>`_ can also provide information on what's possible via different publishing methods.
